Let's Make Some Brownies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.
  2. In a large saucepan. melt 1/2 cup butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into preparded pan.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.
  4. Remove brownies from the oven, and make frosting. Combine 3 tablespoons softened butter, 3 tablespoons cocoa, honey, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, and 1 cup confectioners' sugar. Stir until smooth.
  5. Frost brownies while they are still warm.
